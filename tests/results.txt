Sometimes we've been losing connection to one or otehr of the USB devices. We can see teh device but it stops responding to us and we're not sure why this is. It only seems to effect the GSUSB devices and only after a connection has been previously. We would guess that some data was still in teh buffer that needed reading and it wasn't so it caused the device to cease to function correctly. This can be addressed in a future driver update.
